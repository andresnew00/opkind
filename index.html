Why Operation Kindness Humane Society

North Texas' Pioneer No-Kill Shelter, Trusted Since 1976

Since a group of passionate volunteers opened their doors in 1976, Operation Kindness Humane Society has been North Texas' most trusted lifesaving shelter. We receive zero government funding. Every adoption, every surgery, every bag of food in our pantry happens because of people like you. We don't euthanize animals for space or for treatable conditions. Every animal gets the kindness they deserve.

Your Questions, Answered

Everything You Want to Know

Your gift funds the daily operations of our lifesaving shelter: food, veterinary care, vaccines, staff, foster support, and community programs like our pet food pantry. We publish annual financial reports and hold a Charity Navigator 4-Star rating, so you can always see exactly where the money goes.
Yes. There is no contract and no commitment period. You can cancel, pause, or change your monthly amount at any time by contacting us. We make it simple because we want you to give with complete peace of mind.
Yes. Operation Kindness Humane Society is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (Tax ID #75-1553350). Your monthly gifts are fully tax-deductible and you will receive a receipt for your records.
No. We receive zero government funding. Every single dollar that pays for an animal's surgery, food, or shelter comes from generous donors like you. This makes your monthly gift incredibly important to everything we do.
We will not euthanize an animal for space or for a treatable medical condition. Euthanasia is considered only as a last act of mercy for animals suffering from irredeemable medical conditions, after all other options are exhausted. Every animal gets a real chance at a loving home.
Every Kindness Keeper receives monthly impact updates with real stories from the shelter. Animal Guardian and Lifesaving Champion members receive quarterly photo updates of specific animals their gift helped. You'll always know your money is at work.
Our Shelter Friend tier starts at just $15 per month. That's less than a streaming subscription and it funds real, ongoing care for animals in need. Every amount makes a meaningful difference when it comes in reliably each month.
The Kindness Keepers is our monthly giving community. Members are the steady, reliable core of support that keeps our shelter running year-round. You'll receive exclusive updates, recognition, and behind-the-scenes access that one-time donors don't get.
Absolutely. Operation Kindness Humane Society accepts honor gifts and memorial gifts. You can set up a monthly tribute gift in someone's name, and we'll acknowledge the dedication appropriately. Visit our Ways to Give page for more details.
